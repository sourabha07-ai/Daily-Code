# Operators in C++

Operators let you manipulate values and compare expressions.

## Arithmetic

- `+` addition
- `-` subtraction
- `*` multiplication
- `/` division
- `%` modulus

## Comparison

- `==` equal to
- `!=` not equal to
- `>` greater than
- `<` less than

## Logical

- `&&` and
- `||` or
- `!` not
